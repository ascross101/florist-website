About

Welcome to our Florist About Page! At Fleur de lis, we have a passion for bringing beauty and joy into people's lives through the art of flowers. Our journey began with a simple dream - to create breathtaking floral arrangements that evoke emotions, inspire connections, and leave lasting impressions. As a family-owned and operated business, we take pride in our commitment to delivering the freshest blooms and exceptional customer service to our valued clients.
